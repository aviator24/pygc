Power law potential¶

$$ \Phi = \frac{1}{2}\Omega_0^2 R_0^2 \left(\frac{1}{1-q}\right)\left(\frac{R}{R_0}\right)^{2-2q} $$ $$ \frac{\partial\Phi}{\partial x} = \Omega_0^2 x \left(\frac{R}{R_0}\right)^{-2q} $$ $$ \frac{\partial\Phi}{\partial y} = \Omega_0^2 y \left(\frac{R}{R_0}\right)^{-2q} $$
